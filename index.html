history of the university

Founded in 2014, Chitoma University stands as a beacon of knowledge and innovation nestled in the vibrant city of Livingstone, Zambia. Its inception was driven by a collective vision to establish a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence, community engagement, and global impact. From its humble beginnings, Chitoma University quickly emerged as a hub for cutting-edge research, dynamic learning experiences, and a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual growth.

Over the years, Chitoma University has solidified its reputation as a center of learning that transcends boundaries and empowers individuals to shape a brighter future. With state-of-the-art facilities and a diverse faculty of esteemed scholars, the university offers a wide range of programs spanning various disciplines, inspiring students to explore, inquire, and innovate. Beyond the classroom, Chitoma University has become an integral part of the Livingstone community, fostering collaborations, hosting cultural events, and contributing to the region's socio-economic development. As it continues to evolve, Chitoma University remains committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and changemakers, ensuring a lasting legacy of academic excellence and positive impact.
