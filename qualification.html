University rules and regulation

  1. Academic Integrity: All members of the university community must uphold the highest standards of academic integrity. Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited.
  2. Code of Conduct: All students, faculty, and staff are expected to adhere to a code of conduct that promotes respectful and ethical behavior. Discrimination, harassment, and any form of disruptive conduct are not tolerated.
  3. Attendance and Participation: Students are required to attend classes and actively participate in academic activities. Instructors may establish attendance policies for their courses.
  4. Dress Code: Appropriate attire should be worn in academic and public spaces on campus. While casual dress is acceptable in many areas, certain events and situations may call for more formal attire.
  5. Campus Safety: The university is committed to providing a safe environment for all members of the community. It is mandatory to comply with safety protocols, emergency evacuation procedures, and to report any suspicious activities promptly.
  6. Respect for Property: All university facilities, equipment, and property should be treated with respect. Vandalism, theft, or misuse of university property is strictly prohibited.
  7. Noise and Disturbances: Students should be considerate of others in shared living spaces and quiet study areas. Excessive noise and disturbances that disrupt the learning environment are not allowed.
  8. Alcohol and Substance Abuse: The university follows local laws and regulations regarding the possession and consumption of alcohol and controlled substances. Illegal drug use, distribution, or excessive alcohol consumption on campus is prohibited.
  9. Parking and Transportation: All vehicles on campus must be registered with the university and adhere to designated parking areas and regulations. Alternative transportation options and environmentally friendly practices are encouraged.
  10. Student Organizations and Events: Recognized student organizations must follow university guidelines for event planning, fundraising, and promotion. All events should be conducted in a manner that upholds the university's values and contributes positively to the campus community.
